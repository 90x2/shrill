* shrill

a utility for switching monitor outputs on sway or X11, with refresh
rate configuration.

requires [[https://github.com/json-c/json-c][json-c]]

** why?

i use a thinkpad, and its display sucks. i have a second monitor that is
marginally better, and i prefer it. my desk is too small to care anyway.

*** why not use the APIs directly?

because i want this thing to work correctly, and to be easily
tweakable. this is, effectively, a wrapper.

*** ...and why a single file?

i think single-file C programs are as beautiful as the haiku or the
shitpost. in an ideal world, i would be a single C file inside of
a computer.
